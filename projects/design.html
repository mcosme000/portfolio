About

On this page I will showcase some of the designs I made in Illustrator in my previous job. I didn't know how to use this tool, but I gained confidence with each of these projects and that's when I started to get interested in design.

Area map

I designed a map for our guests that showcases interesting places in area near our hostel. Since we the hostel is located in Shinjuku, there are plenty of restaurants, shops and other tourist attractions to choose from. It's not only travelers who have trouble choosing places to go, we as staff also spend a lot of time thinking of recommendations when guests come to the reception to ask!

By the time we started using these maps, the interactions with guests improved and the time we spend researching for places or showing them indications drastically decreassed.

You can read in details about this design on Behance
