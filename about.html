Hi, I am María

I'm from Spain and I'm a self-taught frontend developer and UX/UI designer. I currently live in Nara, Japan.

Due to Covid-19, I had to quit my job as a hostel receptionist after almost 4 years. I decided it was time to look for new challenges, so I started learning programming that same year. Last year I also got interested in UX/UI design.

What I like most about this field is not only the designing part, but also getting to know the different users, their needs and objectives, and progressing little by little until I manage to design the best product for each of them.
